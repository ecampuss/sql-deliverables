This query automatically detects errors in fiscal invoices. The query checks for common issues such as missing or invalid tax codes, pricing mismatches, incorrect vendor information, and inconsistencies between expected and reported values. It runs across multiple tables to ensure data integrity and highlights any discrepancies that could impact compliance or delay financial processes. This helps streamline invoice validation, reduce manual review efforts, and ensures quicker resolution of errors, ultimately improving the reliability and efficiency of our billing operations.
According to business department, this query brought more than 300.000 BRL (brazilian reais) per year avoiding errors being issued in the government databases.
