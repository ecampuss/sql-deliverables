THis query extracts a complete snapshot of the fiscal invoice (NF) data and makes it available in a contingency system. This allows users to manually issue invoices based on accurate and up-to-date information in case the ERP system becomes unavailable due to an unexpected outage. The query consolidates all the essential invoice details—such as product data, tax information, customer details, and pricing—so that business continuity is maintained even during system downtimes. It ensures that critical operations like invoice issuance can continue with minimal disruption.
The company avoided implementing a third-party market tool through a new project thanks to this solution that was implemented and, according to the manager who was analysing the feasibility of the new tool, this solution brought a 0,5 million BRL (Brazilian Reais) of savings to the company, considering the acquisition value plus the project expenses.
